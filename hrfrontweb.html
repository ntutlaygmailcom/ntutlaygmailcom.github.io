Introduction

The "HR Recruitment Frontend System" is a job-seeking platform designed specifically for candidates, aiming to provide a smoother and more efficient application experience. Through this system, users can easily browse job openings, upload their resumes, and quickly complete the application process. Clear application progress tracking and real-time notifications make the entire job search journey more transparent and reassuring.
