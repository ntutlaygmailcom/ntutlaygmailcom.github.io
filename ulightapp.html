01.INTRO

This app is designed for U-light 3D printers, enabling real-time remote monitoring. Users can check machine availability, track print progress and remaining time, and remotely pause or stop printing when needed. It also includes memo and task boards for collaboration and issue tracking. With these features, U-light makes 3D printing easier and safer, especially for schools and labs where beginners often use the machines.
